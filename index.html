About Me

My name is Austin Nguyen. I am a sophomore enrolled at the University of Texas at Dallas as a neuroscience major. I enjoy making digital art and playing Apex Legends. I hope to work in graphic design or music production in the future as a side job.

"It’s not the best platform, but I guess it works"

I started using Photoshop back in 7th grade, 7 years ago. I started simple, making some humorous edits of my friends. As time went on, the full scope of Photoshop’s power became clear to me. I started learning new avenues to go about different edits. Sure, Photoshop might not be the best for making art, but it works damn well enough for me.
